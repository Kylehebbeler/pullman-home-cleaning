Terms of Service

By booking a cleaning service or checking the agreement box at checkout, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to these Terms.

1. What We Do

We provide residential cleaning services for homes, apartments, and rental units in Pullman, WA and surrounding areas. Services are performed by our employed cleaning staff. We do not subcontract cleaning work to third parties.

The scope of each service tier (Quick Clean, Whole Home, Deep Clean) is described on our pricing page. We reserve the right to decline service for properties that are excessively soiled beyond what was booked, or that present unsafe conditions for our staff.

2. Booking & Payment

All bookings are completed online through our booking system. Payment is collected at the time of booking via Stripe. We accept major credit and debit cards. All prices are in USD and include applicable taxes.

You must provide a valid email address and phone number at booking. We use these to send confirmation, reminders, and service updates. Providing false contact information may result in cancellation of your booking without refund.

Price displayed at checkout is final. If you believe you were charged incorrectly, contact us at pullmanhomecleaning@gmail.com within 7 days of the charge.

3. Subscriptions & Auto-Renewal

Subscription plans (bi-weekly or monthly) are recurring services. By purchasing a subscription, you authorize the Company to charge your payment method automatically at the selected frequency until you cancel.

  • Bi-weekly: Your card is charged every 14 days starting from your first appointment date.
  • Monthly: Your card is charged on the same calendar date each month starting from your first appointment date.

You will receive a reminder email before each recurring charge. Subscription pricing is the discounted rate shown at checkout; one-time pricing applies if you cancel and re-book outside a subscription.

To cancel a subscription: Email us at pullmanhomecleaning@gmail.com or use the cancellation link in any booking confirmation email at least 24 hours before your next scheduled appointment. Cancellations received less than 24 hours before the next appointment will be charged for that appointment.

We reserve the right to modify subscription pricing with 30 days advance notice to your email address on file.

4. Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • Cancellations with 24+ hours notice: Full refund, no charge.
  • Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice: A fee of 25% of the service price will apply. We cannot guarantee our cleaner can be reassigned on short notice.
  • No-shows (cleaner cannot access the property): A fee of 50% of the service price applies. If our cleaner is unable to enter the property at the scheduled time, we will attempt to contact you. If we cannot reach you within 15 minutes, the appointment is marked as a no-show.
  • Rescheduling: Rescheduling with 24+ hours notice is free and unlimited. Contact us by email or use the link in your confirmation email.

5. Liability & Property Damage

We carry general liability insurance. If our staff damages your property during a cleaning, please report it to us within 24 hours of the appointment by emailing pullmanhomecleaning@gmail.com with photos and a description. Reporting promptly helps us verify the cause — all claims are evaluated on their merits.

Liability cap: Our total liability for any property damage claim is limited to the amount recoverable under our general liability insurance policy. We are not liable for pre-existing damage, items damaged due to their inherent fragility or improper storage, or damage to items not disclosed to us before the cleaning.

We strongly recommend securing irreplaceable items, valuables, jewelry, loose cash, and fragile collectibles before your appointment. We are not responsible for items lost or damaged when they were left unsecured in cleaning areas.

To the fullest extent permitted by Washington law, we are not liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages arising from our services.

6. Your Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate contact information and a correct service address at booking.
  • Ensure our staff can access the property at the scheduled time (key, code, or in-person access).
  • Secure pets in a separate room or outside during the cleaning.
  • Disclose any known hazards, biohazard conditions, or unusually soiled areas before booking so we can assess whether the standard service tier is appropriate.
  • Not request our staff to perform tasks outside the booked service scope (e.g., lifting furniture, exterior cleaning, handling biohazard materials) without prior agreement.

7. Termination

We reserve the right to refuse or discontinue service to any customer for any lawful reason, including but not limited to: unsafe working conditions, abusive conduct toward our staff, repeated no-shows, or disputed chargebacks.
